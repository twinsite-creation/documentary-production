Cinematographer

Iryna
Bondar

Iryna Bondar learned to see before she learned to frame. Growing up in Lviv, she spent years photographing industrial landscapes before discovering that the camera could also hold time — not just space. Her work with light is inseparable from her understanding of loss.

She has shot five features and eleven shorts for MOVIE Studio, developing a signature approach: long exposures, minimal artificial light, and a refusal to aestheticize suffering. Her images are honest precisely because they are restrained.

Iryna studied cinematography at the Łódź Film School and was named one of Documentary Magazine's 25 New Faces of Independent Film in 2021.
