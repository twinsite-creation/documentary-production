Director

Marta
Koval

Marta Koval began her career documenting post-industrial communities in Eastern Ukraine, where she learned that the most powerful stories live in what people choose not to say. Her debut feature earned her the title of one of Europe's most uncompromising documentary voices.

Over twelve years she has worked across four continents, finding in each location a particular kind of silence — the silence that follows historical rupture. Her films do not explain. They witness.

She is a graduate of the Kyiv National I.K. Karpenko-Kary Theatre, Cinema and Television University and has been a fellow at the Sundance Documentary Lab.
